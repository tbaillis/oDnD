The Monster AI has been enhanced to take complete turns:

  • Multiple actions per turn - Move AND attack in same turn
  • Smart decision making - Moves closer when out of range, then attacks
  • Action budget respect - Checks available actions before deciding
  • Complete turn execution - Continues until turn budget exhausted or AI chooses to end

How it works:

  1. Monster AI evaluates current situation
  2. If enemy is too far, uses movement action to get closer
  3. Game state updates, action budget recalculated
  4. If enemy is now in range and standard action available, attacks
  5. Continues until no more desired actions or budget exhausted
  6. Ends turn
